The Challenge

You are an analyst for GoatMe, a conglomerate, that recently aquired McQuery - A quick service restaurant chain known for its Happy Insight Meals, and Python McNuggets.



The directors of sales and operations are intrested in how each store is preforming, as well as how the chain is preforming as a whole.



Use your recently acquired Mode skills to create a report that will either:

  1. For Director of Sales: Provide a wholistic snapshot of McQuery's, or
  2. For Director of Operations: Provide insights into the operations stats of a single location.

Your Report Should Include At Least:

  • 3 Visuaizations of Different Types
  • A Unifying Color Palette
  • 1 Calculated Field
  • 2 Filters
  • 1 Parameter
  • Have a scheduled run cadence

BONUS POINTS

  • Uses a UI Kit(50pts)
  • SQL query uses a Definition(20pts)
  • Python-based Visualization(40pts)
  • Has a Custom Theme(15pts)
  • Finish First(30pts)
